## Purpose

This document is the structured reference for Phase 2.5 (Best Practices & Authoritative Source Research). It contains five layers of authoritative open-access resources organized by domain relevance. Benefit uses this to conduct targeted sweeps of the world’s leading consulting firms, regulatory bodies, and domain specialists.

## How to Use This Document

1. During Phase 2.5, Benefit identifies which layers are relevant based on the product being built
1. For each relevant institution, Benefit searches for published frameworks and best practices related to the product domain
1. Findings are presented to the user with adoption recommendations (Adopt / Adapt / File / Reject)
1. The user decides what enters the product definition — Benefit never auto-incorporates

## Search Strategy Guide

When searching these sources, use these query patterns:

- **For consulting firms:** `site:[domain] [product topic] framework` or `[firm name] [topic] insights [year]`
- **For regulatory bodies:** `site:[domain] [regulation type] guidance` or `[authority] [topic] requirements`
- **For behavioral/actuarial:** `[firm name] [behavioral topic] research` or `[firm name] [insurance topic] best practices`

### Relevance Assessment

After searching, assess each finding on three dimensions:

1. **Applicability** — Does this framework apply to our specific product/user/market?
1. **Recency** — Is this current (published within last 2-3 years)?
1. **Actionability** — Can we translate this into specific product decisions?

Only present findings that score well on all three dimensions. Don’t surface irrelevant research just to demonstrate thoroughness.
