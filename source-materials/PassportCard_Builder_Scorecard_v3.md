## Scoring Criteria

| Criterion | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Content** (1-10) | Amount and depth of published research, frameworks, and data relevant to the domain |
| **Access.** (1-10) | Ease of finding, filtering, and accessing content. Open-access vs. gated; search/filter quality |
| **Recency** (1-10) | How current content is and publishing cadence |
| **Action.** (1-10) | How directly frameworks translate into product decisions for PassportCard |

## Tier System

| Tier | Score Range | Meaning |
|------|-------------|---------|
| ★★★ | 8.5–10.0 | Primary source for Benefit Phase 2.5 sweeps |
| ★★ | 7.0–8.4 | Strong supplementary source |
| ★ | 5.5–6.9 | Niche value, selective search |
| — | below 5.5 | Limited open-access; consider direct engagement |
